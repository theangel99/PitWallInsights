Stats. Strategy. Business.

The best teams don’t guess. They analyze, adapt, and win.

My passion for F1 goes beyond the race itself. I'm fascinated by the numbers, strategy, and business decisions that define the sport. From analyzing tire degradation to understanding how sponsorship deals shape team performance, I love decoding the factors that lead to victory. Motorsport isn’t just about speed—it’s about making the right call at the right time, and data is at the heart of it all
