Ferrari and IBM Join Forces to Transform F1 Fan Engagement

In a groundbreaking move, Scuderia Ferrari has partnered with IBM to revolutionize fan engagement through advanced data analytics and digital innovation. â€‹

A New Era of Fan Interaction

Starting in 2025, this multi-year collaboration aims to bring fans closer to the action than ever before. Central to this initiative is the development of a reimagined mobile application, designed to offer personalized content, real-time insights, and immersive features. By harnessing IBM's expertise in data analytics, the app will transform Ferrari's extensive historical and current data into engaging fan experiences.

Shared Vision and Expertise

IBM's extensive experience in enhancing fan engagement for major sporting events, such as Wimbledon and the US Open, positions them well to elevate the digital experience for Ferrari's global fanbase.

We are proud to launch this partnership with IBM, one of the most prestigious companies in the world offering technology and consulting expertise. The collaboration with IBM will see us offering Scuderia Ferrari HP fans unprecedented experiences that will open the doors of the Scuderia to the outside world.
Lorenzo Giorgetti, Chief Racing Revenue Officer at Ferrari

Anticipating the 2025 Season

As the 2025 Formula 1 season approaches, fans can look forward to a more connected and personalized experience, thanks to this innovative partnership. The fusion of Ferrari's passion for racing with IBM's technological prowess promises to set a new standard in motorsport fan engagement.
